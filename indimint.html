Our Technology

EchoGuard utilizes state-of-the-art FPGA-based architecture for real-time signal processing and analysis. Our proprietary algorithms can distinguish between gunshots and similar sounds, even in noisy urban environments.

The system processes audio inputs from multiple microphones, using advanced time-difference-of-arrival (TDOA) techniques to accurately localize the source of gunshots in 3D space.
